About

Get To Know More

CNPC Chuanqing Drilling Engineering Company Limited (CCDC), headquartered in Chengdu, Sichuan, is a leading subsidiary of CNPC and a major oilfield service provider in China. Established in 2008 through the merger of several petroleum bureaus and technical units, CCDC specializes in drilling engineering, mud logging, cementing, well testing, workover, and oil & gas exploration and production.

With over 27,000 employees and 283 on-shore drilling rigs, CCDC serves key domestic fields like PetroChina Southwest Oil & Gasfield Company, Changqing Oilfield, and Tarim Oilfield, as well as overseas markets including Turkmenistan, Pakistan, and Ecuador.

A national high-tech enterprise, CCDC has developed over 140 proprietary technologies for complex and deep wells, shale gas, and tight oil/gas, securing 2053 patents and multiple national and provincial awards. The company emphasizes safety, environmental protection, and social responsibility while striving to become a comprehensive oil and gas service provider with leading engineering capabilities.
